Core Technology

Cryptographic Stack

01
🔐
SHA-256 Hashing

Every document generates a unique 256-bit cryptographic fingerprint. Any modification — even a single byte — produces an entirely different hash, making tampering instantly detectable.

ALG: SHA-256 / FIPS 180-4
02
✍️
RSA Digital Signatures

4096-bit RSA key pairs bind document identity to its origin. Each signature is cryptographically unique to the issuer, providing non-repudiation and irrefutable authorship proof.

ALG: RSA-4096 / PKCS#1 v2.2
03
🛡️
AES-256 Encryption

Military-grade symmetric encryption protects document payloads in transit and at rest. The same encryption standard used by governments and defense agencies worldwide.

ALG: AES-256-GCM / NIST SP 800-38D
04
📱
QR-Based Verification

Compact, scannable QR codes embed the complete verification payload — hash, signature, timestamp, and metadata — enabling instant offline verification from any device.

SPEC: QR ISO/IEC 18004:2015
Process

How It Works

01
Upload Document

Drag and drop any PDF. Our system immediately begins cryptographic processing.

02
Hash Generation

SHA-256 generates an immutable fingerprint of the exact document contents.

03
Sign & Encrypt

RSA private key signs the hash. AES-256 encrypts the verification payload.

04
QR Seal

A tamper-proof QR code is generated, embedding the complete cryptographic proof.

05
Instant Verify

Anyone can scan the QR or re-upload the document to verify authenticity in seconds.
